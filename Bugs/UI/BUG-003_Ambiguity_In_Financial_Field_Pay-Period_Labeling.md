ID: BUG-003
Title: Ambiguity in financial field pay-period labeling
Type: Bug / UX
Priority: Medium
Severity: Medium

Affected Fields:

Salary

Gross Pay

Benefits Cost

Net Pay

Expected Result:

Each field should clearly indicate whether the amount is:

Annual, or

Per paycheck

Actual Result:

Salary is annual, while Gross Pay, Benefits Cost, and Net Pay are per paycheck

No labels or indicators clarify this distinction

Business Impact:

User confusion

Risk of financial misinterpretation
